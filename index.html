Upcoming

Upcoming events for March 2023!

17th

Limerick St. Patrick's Festival 2023

The fun and craic associated with the Limerick St. Patrick’s Festival makes a welcome return to the city streets from 17th to 19th March 2023. The Limerick St. Patrick’s Festival will include the traditional St. Patrick’s Day Parade on Friday 17th March and a host of other family-friendly events.

19th

51st Limerick International Band Championship

The 51st edition of the International Band Championship will see the streets of Limerick filled with the spectacular sight and sound of talented marching band musicians from across Ireland, Europe and America for Ireland’s only International Band Championship at 12pm on Sunday 19th March 2023. Cheer on the bands as they march through the city competing to be crowned International Band Champions!

25th

Munster Rugby V Glasgow Warriors

Munster take on rivals The Glasgow Warriors in a clash of the titans event at Thomond Park!
